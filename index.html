Hello

Greetings Aviators.

Team Arcis is the Official Aero Design of Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering, Bengaluru. ARCIS means “enlightenment” in Sanskrit, and that is what we strive to achieve in our work with an enlightened approach to aero design. We participate in two of the biggest student design competitions, held annually in the USA. The team acts as an interdisciplinary organization having students from all engineering clusters. We are India's Leading Aero Design Team since 2015. Team Arcis functions as a start-up, raising funds via sponsorships and collaborations, and as per the rules of the competition. One of the most significant achievements of the team was securing the first position in the flight missions and third place in the technical report from the many teams that took part in AIAA-DBF 2021, which was also the second year of the team's participation in that competition.




Vision And Mission

To excel as a team of innovative engineers, leveraging UAV, UAS and other modern technologies for solving real world problems.



To inculcate discipline as well as professional skills in students so that they become responsible citizens.
